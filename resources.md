## Cosmological Backreaction & Averaging

* **[On Average Properties of Inhomogeneous Fluids in General Relativity](https://arxiv.org/abs/gr-qc/9906059)** *Thomas Buchert (1999)*. The foundational paper deriving the scalar averaging equations (Buchert Equations). It establishes the theoretical framework for how local inhomogeneities can manifest as an effective global cosmological evolution.

* **[Backreaction in Late-Time Cosmology](https://arxiv.org/abs/1112.5335)** *Thomas Buchert and Syksy Räsänen (2012)*. A definitive review of the "Averaging Problem." This paper provides the most salient overview of how non-linear structure formation interacts with the background expansion of the universe.
